**__Commands Overview__**
1. __Warning Command:__

``[,warn [user id] Reason]``

``[,removewarn <case id>]``

``[,warnings user id]``

``[,modlogs user id]``

Issues a warning to a user. The reason for the warning should be provided after the user ID.

2. __Timeout / Mute Command:__

``[,mute [user id] (limit 1h, 6h, 1d, etc.) Reason]``

Temporarily mutes or timeout  a user. The duration is flexible (e.g., 1 hour, 6 hours, 1 day) and a reason for the timeout is required.

3. __Remove Timeout / Unmute Command:__
``[?Removetimeout or ?rto/?unmute [user id] Reason]``

Removes the timeout or unmutes the user, with an explanation for the action.

3. __Ban Command:__
``[?ban [user id] Reason]``

Bans a user permanently with a given reason.

4. __Unban Command:__
``[?unban [user id] Reason]``

Unbans a previously banned user with a reason for lifting the ban.

__Additional Note:__
Time-limited bans should only be used for temporary punishments, while normal bans (permanent) should be for more severe or long-term issues

# **__Reports/giveaway ticket management.__**
1. __Greeting:__ Good day, [user]. How may I assist you today?

2. __Inquire about the issue:__ Could you please provide more details regarding your request?

3. __Address the ticket:__

If it's a report, I will request evidence. Example: Please provide the required evidence to help us investigate.

If it's a giveaway claim or buissness inquiry, I will redirect the ticket to the giveaway host/owner. Example: I will forward your ticket to the owner for your claim/inquiry.

If it requires escalation, I will escalate the issue. Example: I will escalate this matter to a higher-level team member for further assistance.

4. __Before closing:__ I hope I was able to assist you. Should you have any further questions, feel free to reach out. Have a great day!

5. __Close the ticket__

6. __Save Transcript:__ The transcript will be saved for record-keeping.

7. __Delete the Ticket:__ The ticket will now be deleted.

**__Promotions and Demotions__**

1. __Promotion__

To be **considered for promotion** to a higher position, active participation within the server is required. **This includes** consistent moderation efforts and the ability to assist lower-ranking staff effectively when needed. The following are the current positions within the team:

**__Owner__**
The highest-ranking individual, responsible for the overall management of the server.

**__Manager__**
Oversees staff management and the community, ensuring smooth operations. 

**__Deputy Head of staff__**
Helps the Managers in activity checks, provide suggestions, etc.

**__Administration__**
Provides guidance to the team and assists the in daily activities.

**__Senior Moderator__**
An experienced moderator who mentors the Moderators and provides leadership in moderation tasks.

**__Moderator__**
Responsible for maintaining the community’s environment, ensuring it remains safe and welcoming for all members.

**__Junior Moderator__**
Staff members which showcased appreciable skills at Trial period and will be working as usual staff in the team. (Cannot handle appeal tickets.)

**__Trial Moderator__**
A staff member undergoing a trial period to assess their skills and compatibility with the team.

**Demotions are determined** at the discretion of the Manager and Owner. **The decision is based on** your activity level, adherence to guidelines, and the responsible use of your powers. Misconduct, including the misuse of power or failure to follow protocol, may lead to demotion.

**__Important Note:__** **The leaking of sensitive staff information or the misuse of authority can result in a demotion. If you observe any staff member engaging in such behavior, please report it immediately to Owner or managers via direct message or staff report.**

**__Staff Moderation Policy - 3-Strike System__**

To maintain professionalism, trust, and accountability within our staff team, this 3-strike system outlines how staff conduct is monitored and enforced. This applies to all members of the moderation team _including managers and adminstration team_.

**Strike System Overview**

**Strike 1: __Verbal or Written Warning__**

- Issued for minor infractions or first-time offenses (includes inactivity, moderation mistakes, behavior.)
- Staff member receives a reminder of expectations and constructive feedback.
- The warning is documented privately by the __Admin team.__

**Strike 2: __Logged Strike__**

- Issued after a repeat offense or a more serious one-time infraction. (includes inactivity, moderation mistakes, behavior, any sort of rule breaking.)
- A formal meeting is required with __senior mod__ and above to address the behavior.
- Strike is officially logged in staff records.

**Strike 3: __Temporary Demotion or Removal__**

- A third offense results in temporary removal from staff duties. (includes inactivity, moderation mistakes, behavior, repeated rule breaking.)
- The staff member may request a talk with __Management team__ to discuss reinstatement, but this is not guaranteed.                                                        
**Instant Demotion or Ban (Bypasses Strike System)**

Certain severe actions result in immediate demotion or permanent removal from staff — no warnings or strikes required:

- **Leaking confidential staff information**, including private channels, messages, logs, or internal decisions.
- **Abuse of power**, such as banning users without valid cause, unjustified deletions, or role misuse.

**__Additional Notes__**

- Strikes **expire after 20 days** of consistent good conduct, unless otherwise specified.
- All warnings, strikes, and removals are **documented by the __Admin team__** for transparency and record-keeping.
- Staff members may **appeal any disciplinary action** through a formal review process with the __Management team.__
